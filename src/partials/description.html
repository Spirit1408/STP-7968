Game Description

Blaze of Battle combines intense strategic gameplay with exciting real-time battles.
Players lead their armies into action against formidable enemies across a variety of stunning battlefields.

Collect powerful heroes, train your troops, and forge alliances to dominate the battlefield.
Master the art of combat and become the ultimate warlord.
Engage in fierce skirmishes, build your empire, and rise to the top as the most feared commander.

Whether you're launching massive assaults or defending your territory, Blaze of Battle offers endless tactical depth
and thrilling combat!
