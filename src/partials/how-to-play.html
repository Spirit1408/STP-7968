How to Play

  • Heroic Leadership

    Recruit and upgrade legendary heroes with powerful skills to lead your troops to victory.

  • Command Your Army

    Deploy a variety of units, including infantry, cavalry, and siege weapons, each with unique abilities.

  • Fortress Defense

    Strengthen your base and defenses to withstand enemy raids.

  • Strategic Battles

    Plan your moves carefully, using terrain and unit advantages to defeat enemies.

  • Alliance Building

    Form alliances with other players to tackle difficult challenges and conquer new territories together.
